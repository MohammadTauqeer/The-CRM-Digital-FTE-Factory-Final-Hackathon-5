# TechCorp Product Documentation

## Account Access
- **Login:** Users can log in via email and password or Google OAuth.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Recommended for all users; can be enabled in Security Settings.
- **Password Reset:** Automated reset links sent via email.

## Pricing Plans
*Note: Prices are firm. Agents are not authorized to negotiate discounts.*

1. **Starter Plan:** $15/month
   - 5 Projects
   - Unlimited Invoices
   - Basic Reporting
2. **Professional Plan:** $45/month
   - Unlimited Projects
   - Priority Support
   - Advanced Analytics
   - Multi-currency support
3. **Enterprise Plan:** $150/month
   - Custom Integration
   - Dedicated Account Manager
   - Audit Logs

## Refund Policy
- **30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:** Full refund for first-time subscribers within the first 30 days of the initial purchase.
- **Monthly Subscriptions:** No refunds for partial months after the initial 30 days.
- **Annual Subscriptions:** Pro-rated refunds available only within the first 6 months, minus a 20% processing fee.
- **Disputed Charges:** Please contact support before initiating a chargeback to avoid account suspension.
